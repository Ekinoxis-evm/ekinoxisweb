# Ekinoxis Labs — Website Structure Brief
> For design reference. Focus on structure, content, and purpose — not current visual style.

---

## About the Brand

**Ekinoxis Labs** is the first Innovation Laboratory of the Colombian Pacific.
- Based in Cali, Valle del Cauca, Colombia (primary) + Casper, Wyoming, USA (secondary)
- Tagline: **"Innovation Without Frontiers"**
- Focus: Blockchain, Cryptography, and Artificial Intelligence
- Audience: Developers, enterprises, universities, governments — primarily LATAM
- Bilingual: English + Spanish (full site)

## Key UX Notes for Design

- Every page must have a clear bilingual toggle (EN / ES)
- Primary CTA across the site is community entry → Telegram
- Secondary CTA is consultation booking → Google Calendar
- Products and hackers are the two most content-rich pages
- Tech Stack page is interactive (category filter/tabs)
- Services/consultation page has multiple pricing tiers
- All team members have individual social links
- Logo files exist for all partner organizations, hackathons, and tech tools
